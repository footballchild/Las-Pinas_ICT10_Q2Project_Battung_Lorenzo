About Our institution

La Masia, FC Barcelona's famed youth academy, has a rich history of nurturing football talent since its establishment in 1979. Renowned for producing world-class players like Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernandez, and Andres Iniesta. Not only does this academy focus on sports development, it also supports education in order for their stuents to have a well balanced life. La Masia emphasizes a holistic approach to player development, combining rigorous football training with academic education. The academy's philosophy centers on technical skill, tactical understanding, and fostering a strong team ethic, all while instilling the club's values of humility, respect, and dedication.

The new facility includes living quarters (bedrooms, dining, leisure), educational spaces (classrooms, study rooms), and extensive sports facilities (gyms, water zone, training pitches) for comprehensive athlete development.
